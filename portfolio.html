Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer a free 30-minute consultation to discuss your project requirements, understand your goals, and determine how we can help. This helps us provide you with an accurate proposal tailored to your needs.

What's your pricing model?

We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials pricing models depending on project scope. For well-defined projects, we provide fixed-price quotes. For more complex or evolving projects, we recommend our hourly rate with regular budget updates.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a custom web application could take 3-6 months. During our consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide maintenance after launch?

Absolutely. We offer various maintenance packages ranging from basic security updates to full-service maintenance with performance monitoring, regular backups, and content updates. Many clients find our ongoing support invaluable.

What technologies do you work with?

Our stack includes modern technologies like React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, and various CMS platforms. We choose technologies based on project requirements, always prioritizing performance, scalability, and long-term maintainability.
